# Privacy Policy for Valley Computers

**Last Updated: February 2025**

Valley Computers ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your information when you use the Valley Computers Android application (the "App").

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Personal Information
We may collect personal information that you provide directly to us, including:
*   **Account Information**: Name, email address, phone number, and physical address when you create a profile or submit a request.
*   **Request Details**: Specific information regarding ISP sign-ups, repair requests, or network infrastructure plans.

### B. Automatically Collected Information (Firebase)
The App uses Firebase services provided by Google to improve our service:
*   **Firebase Analytics**: Collects data on App usage, such as screens visited and actions taken, to help us optimize the user experience.
*   **Firebase Authentication**: Manages secure sign-in and user identity.
*   **Firebase Firestore**: Stores your account profile and service requests securely in the cloud.

## 2. Permissions Used by the App
The App requests the following permissions to provide its core services:
*   **INTERNET & ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE**: Required to communicate with Firebase services and process your requests.
*   **CALL_PHONE**: Allows you to dial our support line directly from the App. No calls are made without your explicit interaction.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We use the information collected to:
*   Process and respond to your service requests (ISP, Repairs, Networking).
*   Maintain your user profile for faster future bookings.
*   Analyze App performance and user behavior to improve our services.
*   Communicate with you via WhatsApp or Email regarding your requests.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We share data with Google (Firebase) only to the extent necessary to provide App functionality and analytics. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

## 5. Data Security
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## 6. Your Choices
You can update your profile information at any time within the **Account** section of the App. You can also contact us to request the deletion of your data.
